Why NUTN

Why Choose NUTN

A century of teacher-education heritage meets the vision of a modern comprehensive university. NUTN offers a solid yet open stage for learning — at an affordable cost, an intimate scale and with cross-disciplinary energy.

01

A Century of Heritage

Born as Taiwan's first normal school, NUTN holds over a century of depth in education, special education and the arts — a foundation few institutions can match.

02

Public, Excellent, Affordable

As a national university, tuition is around NT$60,000 a year — far below private institutions — for high-quality teaching and facilities.

03

An Intimate University

With around 6,500 students and over 200 full-time faculty, the student–faculty ratio is excellent: professors see and support every student.

04

Six Colleges, One Campus

Education × Technology × Arts × Management. Cross-disciplinary programs, minors and double majors keep your learning free of silos.

05

The Heart of Old Tainan

Set in Taiwan's oldest cultural capital — rich in heritage and food, with excellent amenities and a low cost of living. Study and life in balance.

06

Five Core Competencies

Built around character, self-learning, creativity, communication and employability — educating whole people ready for the future.

Flagship Programs

Departments That Lead the Nation

These programs are NUTN's signature — some carrying a century-old teacher-education tradition, others at the forefront of educational technology and sustainability.

教
College of Education

Department of Education

NUTN's founding strength and flagship in teacher education. A full ladder from bachelor's to doctorate, covering curriculum & instruction, educational management, educational data analytics and instructional technology.

特
College of Education

Department of Special Education

One of Taiwan's most complete special-education programs. With a master's in assistive technology and close ties to the affiliated School for the Hearing Impaired, it offers practicum settings few schools can match.

數
Science & Engineering

Department of Digital Learning Technology

Where education meets technology. Focused on learning technology, digital courseware, educational data mining and interactive learning — bachelor's through doctorate — producing job-ready talent for digital education.

藝
College of Arts

Visual Arts & Design · Music · Drama Creation & Application

Inheriting a deep artistic tradition from its normal-school roots, three departments advance together — from visual design and musical performance to theatre creation and applied drama — a key cradle of artists in southern Taiwan.

理
Science & Engineering × Environment

Computer Science & Information Engineering · Green Energy

Rising departments aligned with AI, cybersecurity and net-zero sustainability. Theory and industry partnership in equal measure, from software and security to green-energy technology.

文
College of Humanities

Cultural & Natural Resources · Chinese · English

The old capital as a classroom. Cultural tourism, Taiwan studies, language teaching and cross-cultural communication are practiced in the field across Tainan — building humanists with local insight and global language skills.

管
College of Management

Business & Management · Public Administration

The youngest college, founded in 2016 — uniting business, technology management and public administration, with an EMBA program bridging talent needs across the public and private sectors.

Student Benefits

At NUTN, These Have Your Back

Affordable tuition, housing subsidies, diverse aid and exceptional practicum resources — so you can focus on learning and growth.

Affordable Public Tuition

Undergraduate tuition is around NT$60,000 a year, state-subsidized — far lighter on families than private institutions.

Housing Subsidies

Under the Ministry of Education's scheme, up to NT$5,000 per semester (NT$7,000 for students in need); dorms from about NT$16,800–20,000 a year.

Diverse Scholarships

Dean's-list awards, campus scholarships, need-based aid and emergency relief — rewarding study and removing barriers.

Built-in Practicum Sites

An affiliated primary school, high school and School for the Hearing Impaired give education students exceptional teaching-practice resources.

Two Convenient Campuses

The Fucheng (Central West) and Rongyu (East) campuses both sit near the city center — easy transit, full amenities, affordable rent.

Whole-Person Employability

Five-competency courses, teacher-education programs and industry partnerships connect theory to the workplace by graduation.

*Tuition, housing and subsidy amounts are subject to the university's annual official announcements.

Heritage · Tainan

The Red Building. Old Tainan. A Century of Learning.

The campus "Red Building," completed in 1922, is a three-storey clay-brick structure and a designated municipal heritage site — the former main hall of Tainan Normal School. A wall of bullet holes from WWII air raids is preserved on its north face, a quiet witness to the years.

Walk the Fucheng campus and red brick, old pines and the sound of study intertwine. To study in Taiwan's oldest cultural city is to make history part of daily life.
